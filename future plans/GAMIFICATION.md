# Identity-Building Gamification System

This document outlines the 15 personalized, utility-driven gamification mechanics built into Taskflow. Unlike standard "labels" or "badges," each of these mechanics provides a functional utility reward designed to intercept and reprogram specific psychological and productivity blocks.

---

## Group 1: Focus & Momentum
*Targeting the inability to start, morning grogginess, and the "90% done" trap.*

### 1. ⚡ Laser Overdrive (Focus Momentum)
* **Target Problem**: Lack of sustained focus; the desire to build momentum.
* **How it's earned**: Complete a 50-minute Deep Work focus session with zero "distractions" logged.
* **The Reward**: Activates **Laser Overdrive** for the next 2 hours.
* **Utility**: All XP earned from finishing tasks is doubled (2x). The UI adopts a high-contrast "Focus Glow" to signify the active buff, encouraging the user to "stack" smaller tasks while the multiplier is active.

### 2. 🧤 The Closer (Task Completion)
* **Target Problem**: The "90% done" trap—solving the hard part of a project and abandoning the remaining easy work until deadline panic.
* **How it's earned**: Finish the final subtask of any parent task.
* **The Reward**: The final subtask yields **5x the standard XP**.
* **Utility**: Completing the task 100% grants a **Momentum Surge** buff (reduces "Focus Drain" visual UI elements for 4 hours), shifting the dopamine hit from the discovery phase to the actual finish line.

### 3. 🐦 Morning Lark Incentive (Early Starts)
* **Target Problem**: Inability to start working until late morning (10:30 AM); morning grogginess.
* **How it's earned**: Complete the first task or focus session of the day before 9:30 AM.
* **The Reward**: Grants the **Zen Mode** buff for 3 hours.
* **Utility**: Hides all non-essential dashboard statistics, long-term goals, and "Avoided" task counts. It creates a special "bonus round" early in the day, reducing morning overwhelm.

## Group 2: Emotional & Mastery
*Targeting fear-based paralysis, CBT tracking, and long-term consistency.*

### 4. 🛡️ Streak Shield (Habit Resilience)
* **Target Problem**: The "All or nothing" mentality; anxiety over breaking a long streak.
* **How it's earned**: Maintain any daily habit for 10 consecutive days.
* **The Reward**: Grants one **Streak Shield** to the user's inventory.
* **Utility**: The next time a day is missed for that habit, the shield is passively consumed to "freeze" the streak, preventing it from resetting to zero.

### 5. 🌀 Prediction Crystals (CBT Mastery)
* **Target Problem**: Intolerance of uncertainty; anxiety and intrusive thoughts.
* **How it's earned**: When a logged "Worry" resolves and the user confirms the "Worry Accuracy" was low (i.e., the bad outcome didn't happen).
* **The Reward**: Grants a **Clarity Crystal** to the inventory.
* **Utility**: Manually consumed by the user to "dim the noise." For 1 hour, all red "Urgent" UI indicators and overdue badges are hidden across the application.

### 6. 🎭 Poker Face Composure (Anxiety Management)
* **Target Problem**: Cortisol freeze response when facing high-stakes work.
* **How it's earned**: Starting a Focus Session on a "High Priority" task within 5 minutes of opening the app.
* **The Reward**: Grants a **Composure Coin**.
* **Utility**: Can be spent to "Silently Snooze" any task's deadline by 2 hours without it registering as a "Push" (avoiding guilt/avoidance metrics).

## Group 3: Growth & Identity
*Targeting shame, social sharing paradoxes, and physical self-care.*

### 7. 🧠 Mistake Mastery (Growth Mindset)
* **Target Problem**: Shame surrounding procrastination and pushed tasks.
* **How it's earned**: Logging an entry in the "Mistake & Feedback Log" detailing why a task failed or was pushed.
* **The Reward**: Grants a **Fresh Start Token**.
* **Utility**: Consumed on a highly procrastinated task to reset its `pushCount` to 0 and remove its "Overdue" visual styling, allowing a guilt-free fresh start.

### 8. 🤐 The Silent Architect (Execution over Talk)
* **Target Problem**: The Talk vs. Action paradox; fear of announcing plans and public failure.
* **How it's earned**: A passive multiplier that grows the longer a Goal or Task remains in "Private Mode" (no Jots, no sharing).
* **The Reward**: Securing the **Master of Execution** badge upon completion of a silent goal.
* **Utility**: Increases the XP reward for the next 5 tasks by 50%.

### 9. 🥗 Metabolic Fueling (Physical/Mental Link)
* **Target Problem**: Being underweight; forgetting to eat; brain fog.
* **How it's earned**: A "Vitality Check-in" appears after 60 minutes of deep focus.
* **The Reward**: Confirming hydration or eating grants the **Brain Fuel** buff.
* **Utility**: Increases the user's "T-Shirt Accuracy XP" temporarily, directly linking physical self-care to cognitive reward outputs in the app.

## Group 4: Advanced Strategy & Planning
*Targeting backlog displacement, minimum effort defaults, and the planning fallacy.*

### 10. ⚓ The Anchor (Backlog Management)
* **Target Problem**: Cascading task displacement; new tasks burying old ones.
* **How it's earned**: Checking off 3 tasks from the "oldest pending" list before adding any new tasks for the day.
* **The Reward**: Grants an **Anchor Weight**.
* **Utility**: When activated, forces a "Backlog Lockdown" for 2 hours. The app visually hides recent tasks and disables the global "Add Task" button, forcing focus on the backlog.

### 11. 🚀 The Pinnacle Push (Reaching Higher)
* **Target Problem**: Aiming for the minimum; inconsistent confidence.
* **How it's earned**: Voluntarily upgrading a task's T-shirt size estimate (e.g., M to L) to do a better, more complete job, and finishing it within time.
* **The Reward**: Grants a **Stretch Token**.
* **Utility**: Unlocks a "Secret Mode" multiplier on long-term Milestones, granting 3x XP for pushing beyond the minimum viable completion.

### 12. ⏱️ The Time Bender (Accurate Estimation)
* **Target Problem**: The Planning Fallacy; deep-diving over-research.
* **How it's earned**: Finishing a task exactly when your estimated timebox expires (e.g., within 5 minutes of a 60m estimate).
* **The Reward**: Grants a **Time-Bender Hourglass**.
* **Utility**: Spent to automatically shift an upcoming missed deadline forward by 24 hours without triggering an "Overdue" status penalty.

### 13. 🌙 The Twilight Lock (Sleep Discipline)
* **Target Problem**: Broken sleep schedules; staying up until 2 AM.
* **How it's earned**: Completing the nightly "Wind Down" chore before 11:30 PM for 3 consecutive nights.
* **The Reward**: Grants a **Dawn Diamond**.
* **Utility**: Provides an **Energy Injection** buff the following morning. Transforms all "Low Energy" (brainless admin) tasks into High-XP tasks for 2 hours to rapidly build momentum on groggy mornings.

### 14. 📝 Active Synthesizer (Intentional Consumption)
* **Target Problem**: Passive consumption vs. intentional engagement.
* **How it's earned**: Logging a "synthesis/idea" Jot immediately following a "learning/reading" focus session.
* **The Reward**: Grants a **Golden Bookmark**.
* **Utility**: Consumed during moments of "Brain Fog" to randomly surface one highly relevant item from the "Back of Mind" store, pinning it to the dashboard with double XP attached.

### 15. 🕯️ The Ember of Continuity (Burnout Protection)
* **Target Problem**: Losing motivation over long periods; stress hormones lingering.
* **How it's earned**: Logging 5 "Daily Wins" in a row during a self-flagged "High Stress/Uncertainty" period.
* **The Reward**: Grants the **Ember of Continuity**.
* **Utility**: Acts as a permanent "Save State." If the user abandons the app for weeks due to extreme stress, consuming the Ember upon return reverses all streak decay and inactivity penalties perfectly.

## 📅 Roadmap & TODO: Reward System Scaling

To ensure the gamification system remains permanently engaging and doesn't suffer from "XP Inflation" or "Inventory Bloat," the following mechanics need to be implemented in future cycles:

### 1. Dynamic Point Decay & Resets
* [ ] **Weekly XP Normalization**: Implement a soft weekly reset or "Weekly League" where XP earned each week ranks the user against their own historical averages.
* [ ] **Inventory Caps**: Limit the maximum number of overpowered tokens (e.g., maximum 3 *Fresh Start Tokens* held at once) to force the user to actually *use* them rather than hoard them.
* [ ] **Buff Cooldowns**: Ensure buffs like *Laser Overdrive* have a 24-hour internal cooldown to prevent users from grinding unrealistic focus sessions purely for multipliers.

### 2. Streak Maintenance & Continuity
* [ ] **Grace Periods**: If a user misses a habit on a Sunday, give them until Monday 10:00 AM to retroactively check it off before permanently breaking the streak or forcing a *Streak Shield* consumption.
* [ ] **Milestone Pledges**: Allow users to "bet" their XP or stock of *Composure Coins* that they will maintain a streak for exactly 30 days. Winning the bet doubles the investment; losing burns it.

### 3. Progressive Item Acquisition Rates (Loot Scaling)
* [ ] **Diminishing Returns**: To prevent farming, if a user resolves 10 worries in one day, the drop rate for *Prediction Crystals* decreases drastically until the following day.
* [ ] **Level-Gated Rarities**: As the user's total level increases, introduce advanced composite tokens (e.g., combining 3 *Composure Coins* + 1 *Streak Shield* into a *Titanium Will* token).
### 4. Neurochemical Trigger Optimization
* [ ] **Bonsai Visual Evolution (Dopamine)**: Link physical tree growth stages to task-specific chemistry (e.g., "Frog" completion grows flowers).
* [ ] **"The Spotlight" Focus Mode (Acetylcholine)**: A UI state that completely hides the dashboard and sidebar, showing only the current task title and timer to bridge focus.
* [ ] **"Surf the Urge" Timer (GABA)**: A 90-second delay button for impulse control when the user feels the need to check social media or terminate a session.
* [ ] **Shadow Preview (Melatonin)**: Automate a "Wind-Down" UI shift at a set bedtime that hides upcoming stressors and prompts a "Brain Dump" to prep for sleep.
* [ ] **Daily Win Log (Serotonin)**: A non-numeric retrospective at the end of each day to capture identity-affirming successes independent of XP.
* [ ] **Efficiency Pattern Visualization (Glutamate)**: A dashboard widget showing exactly which hours of the day yield the highest focus scores ("Flow Windows").
* [ ] **"Scary Bonus" Scaling (Endorphins)**: Double all XP and item drop rates for tasks tagged with "Dread" or "Anxiety" during the pre-task check-in.
